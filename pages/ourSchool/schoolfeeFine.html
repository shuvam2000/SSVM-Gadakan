School Fee & Fine Policy

1. Payment Schedule
School fees are payable in advance at the beginning of each academic term/semester/year.
2. Fee Structure
The school fee structure includes tuition fees, transportation fees (if applicable), extracurricular activity fees, and any other applicable charges.
3. Payment Methods
Payments can be made through various methods, including online payment portals, bank transfers, or in-person payments at the school's administrative office.
4. Late Payment Penalties
A late fee penalty will be imposed for payments made after the specified deadline.
5. Fine for Non-Payment
Failure to pay school fees within the stipulated period may result in additional fines or penalties.
6. Financial Assistance
The school offers financial assistance or scholarships to eligible students based on predetermined criteria.
7. Refund Policy
Requests for fee refunds due to withdrawal or other reasons will be considered according to the school's refund policy.
8. Transparency and Communication
The school administration is committed to providing transparent information regarding fees, fines, and financial matters.
9. Dispute Resolution
In the event of disputes or concerns regarding school fees or fines, parents/guardians are encouraged to contact the school administration for resolution through dialogue and mediation.
10. Compliance
Compliance with the school fee and fine policy is mandatory for all students and parents/guardians.
