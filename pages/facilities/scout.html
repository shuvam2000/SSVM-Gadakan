Scout and Guide

Scout Program

The Scout program at our school aims to develop leadership skills, outdoor proficiency, and character traits among students through various adventurous activities and community service projects.

Our Scouts participate in camping trips, hiking expeditions, first aid training, and environmental conservation initiatives. These experiences help them build teamwork, resilience, and a sense of responsibility towards society.

Join our Scout program and embark on a journey of self-discovery, adventure, and service!

Guide Program

The Guide program at our school empowers girls to develop leadership skills, self-confidence, and a sense of community service through a variety of engaging activities and projects.

Our Guides participate in outdoor adventures, skill-building workshops, leadership training, and volunteer work. They learn essential life skills, such as communication, problem-solving, and teamwork, in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Join our Guide program and become part of a vibrant community of young leaders making a positive impact!
