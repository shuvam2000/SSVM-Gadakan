School Rules

Contractual Posts

In compliance with the school's hiring policies, contractual posts refer to temporary employment positions within the institution. These posts are typically offered for a specified duration or project, with terms and conditions outlined in the employment contract.

Guidelines for Examination

Examinations are a crucial component of the academic evaluation process. To ensure fairness, integrity, and transparency, the school has established comprehensive guidelines for conducting examinations. These guidelines cover various aspects such as exam scheduling, question paper preparation, invigilation procedures, and result declaration.

Driver Allowance

Drivers employed by the school for transportation purposes are eligible for a monthly driver allowance. This allowance is provided to cover fuel expenses, vehicle maintenance costs, and other related expenses incurred during official duties. The specific amount of the allowance is determined based on prevailing regulations and may be subject to periodic review.

Teaching Staff Qualification

The school maintains high standards of academic excellence by ensuring that teaching staff possess the requisite qualifications and expertise in their respective fields. All teaching staff members are required to hold relevant educational qualifications, certifications, and professional credentials as per regulatory requirements. Continuous professional development and training opportunities are also provided to enhance teaching quality.

Office Order

Office orders serve as official communications issued by the school administration to convey important announcements, decisions, or directives to staff members. These orders may pertain to administrative matters, academic policies, event planning, or any other relevant subjects. Staff members are expected to comply with the instructions outlined in office orders and adhere to prescribed deadlines or guidelines.
